PPC Management stands for Pay-Per-Click-Management. A model of Internet Marketing in which Advertisers Pay a Cost Each Time Someone of Their Ads is Clicked. Essentially, it's a way of buying visits to your site, rather than attempting to "earn" those visits organically. Search engine advertising is one of the most popular forms of PPC.
